About Us

In 2025, I opened The Daily Grind in Gothenburg with a simple vision: to create more than just a place for coffee. I wanted a space where people could pause from the rush of daily life, recharge, and connect.

The idea came from noticing how much of modern life feels like a "grind"—constant deadlines, notifications, and busy routines. I wanted to flip that concept on its head. At The Daily Grind, the focus is on slowing down, enjoying quality coffee, and being present in the moment.

We serve locally roasted beans, fresh pastries, and seasonal, sustainable food, but just as important is the atmosphere: warm, welcoming, and creative. I designed the café as a hub for students, professionals, and artists alike—a place where you can study, hold a meeting, or simply enjoy a quiet corner with your favorite brew.

Starting The Daily Grind was about bringing people together, celebrating Gothenburg’s love for coffee culture, and reminding ourselves that even in the middle of the grind, there’s always room for a break.
